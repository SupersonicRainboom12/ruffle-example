Pac-Xon: Hero Strikes Back!

Controls: Arrow Keys to Move.

Ah, good ol' Pac-Xon. This was nothing like I had ever seen up to this point. Though now I know it's basically a clone of the arcade game Qix, the unique abilities and Pac-Man themeing made me come back to this one over and over. I remember this used to be on a lot of Flash Game websites that hosted other Namco arcade clones, one of them being this Galaga or Galaxian clone with a weird name... Anyone remember that one?
